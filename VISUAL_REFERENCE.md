## Animation Types

### Entrance
- Fade in with y-shift
- Duration: 0.6-0.8s
- Staggered delay: i * 0.1s

### Hover
- Scale: 1.05
- Y-translate: -5px
- Duration: 0.3s

### Scroll Reveal
- Fade in on view
- Y-shift: 20px
- Staggered per item

### Floating
- Infinite Y animation
- Duration: 4-6s
- Amplitude: Вұ10px
